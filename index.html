Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is KPMA, and how is it different from a regular course?

KPMA is a professional creative microcredential bootcamp, not a traditional academic course. Instead of lectures and exams, you spend 1-2 weeks building a real industry-quality project with hands-on guidance from working professionals. You graduate with a portfolio piece employers actually want to see, not just a transcript.

Who is KPMA for?

KPMA is designed for three groups: (1) college students in creative programs (Marketing, Film, Design, Communication) who need portfolio pieces, (2) recent graduates who want to stand out in the job market, and (3) young professionals looking to add Korean creative methodology to their skill set.

Is the certificate recognized?

Yes. The PS Media Professional Microcredential Certificate is QR-verifiable and LinkedIn-compatible. KPMA operates under PinoySeoul Media Enterprise, which has a proven track record with the Korea Pinoy International Film Festival (KPIFF) and a 190,000+ community. The Philippine government (CHED and TESDA) actively supports industry-led microcredential frameworks.

What are the payment options?

We accept GCash (fastest) and bank transfer (BDO, BPI). You can pay in full for a 5% discount, or use the 2-installment plan: 50% upon registration and 50% before Day 1. Cash payments at walk-in rate are accepted on Day 1 if slots remain.

Do I need prior experience?

No. Each track is designed for beginners to intermediate learners. What you need is enthusiasm and willingness to work hard during the intensive. Our mentors guide you through every step, and the cohort size of 30 ensures you get personal attention.

How can my university or department partner with KPMA?

We work directly with professors and department heads. Partnership is zero-cost to the university: students enroll voluntarily, bootcamps can run on your campus on weekends, and faculty receive a Curriculum Consulting Fee for their expert review. Submit an inquiry through our form and we'll set up a conversation.

What is the Seoul Talents Network?

Seoul Talents is PinoySeoul's creator and virtual staffing platform. KPMA serves as the talent incubator: our best graduates receive immediate access to paid brand deals and international freelance opportunities through the network. It's the bridge from your bootcamp output to real paid work.
